Top scorer

Here, all point contributions are combined. In general three categories are counted - serve points, hitting points, and block points. Notice that when a block with more than one blocker is awarded a point, each player receives a whole point.

Player offense

The player offense statistics are based on attacking attempts, attacking points, and attacking errors. The kill and error percentage divides the number of kills or errors with the number of attacking attempts. The kill efficiency substracts the number of errors from the number of kills, and then divides with the number of attacking attempts.
