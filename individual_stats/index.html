Player offense

The player offense statistics are based on attacking attempts, attacking points, and attacking errors. The kill and error percentage divides the number of kills or errors with the number of attacking attempts. The kill efficiency substracts the number of errors from the number of kills, and then divides with the number of attacking attempts.

Player reception

The table shows passing statistics for the passing players (outsides and libero). The error percentage includes serve aces, that happen near the player as well as overpasses, that the opposing team directly kills on the first touch. The positive percentage shows the percentage of the passes of the player, that are counted as either a 2-pass or 3-pass, while the perfect percentage shows the share of 3-passes. Pass rating indicates the average passing rate, ranging from 0 to 3.
